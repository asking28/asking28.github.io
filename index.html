Resource Monitoring System (Cloud Watch)

Prometheus is a powerful, open-source monitoring system that collects metrics from your services and stores them in a time-series database. It offers a multi-dimensional data model, a flexible query language, and diverse visualization possibilities through tools like Grafana. By default, Prometheus only exports metrics about itself (e.g. the number of requests it's received, its memory consumption, etc.). But, you can greatly expand Prometheus by installing exporters, optional programs that generate additional metrics. Exporters — both the official ones that the Prometheus team maintains as well as the community-contributed ones — provide information about everything from infrastructure, databases, and web servers to messaging systems, APIs, and more.

Solar Energy Forecasting

The field of solar and photovoltaic (PV) forecasting is rapidly evolving. The electricity system in India faces several challenges as the energy demand is expected to grow significantly within the next decades while the domestic energy resources in terms of fossil fuels are limited. Hence it becomes important to get more dependent upon Renewable Energy to meet the future requirements. This project report provides state of the art of this dynamic research area, focusing on solar and PV forecast of next dates with given weather data. Diverse resources are used to generate solar and PV forecasts, ranging from measured weather and PV system data to satellite and sky imagery observations of clouds which form the basis of modern weather forecasting. Electric utility companies need accurate forecasts of energy production in order to have the right balance of renewable and fossil fuels available. Errors in the forecast could lead to large expenses for the utility from excess fuel consumption or emergency purchases of electricity from neighboring utilities. Power forecasts typically are derived from numerical weather prediction models, but statistical and machine learning techniques are increasingly being used in conjunction with the numerical models to produce more accurate forecasts.

Smart Energy Metering System

This project presents distinct way of billing and metering system of Energy meters. Electricity Supply Company can take reading of monthly Energy consumption through their mobile phones by just sending SMS (Short Message Service) to GSM (Global System for Mobile) embedded in Energy meter. Features included in our proposed Smart Energy meter are-  Energy Department can take reading using their mobile phone rather than sending meter reader at the end of every month.  They can Cut OFF the Electricity line using Relay by just sending SMS to GSM module and switch it ON again by sending an SMS.  User can continuously monitor their Meter reading of particular month on LCD screen and online by logging into his account.  Website For the User.
