Projects

Financial Narrative Summarization using Pointer Network and T-5

  • Dataset contains Annual Financial reports and each report has 3 to 7 corresponding narrative section reports.
  • We propose our combined extractive and abstractive methodology using PoinT-5 (the combination of Pointer Network and T-5 (Text-to-text transfer Transformer) algorithms) for summarisation.
  • Pointer networks is used to extract important narrative sentences from the report, and then T-5 is used to paraphrase extracted sentences into a concise yet informative sentence.
  • This solution achieves the highest precision scores in all the metrics and highest F1 scores in ROUGE1, and LCS and the only solution to cross the MUSE solution baseline in ROUGE-LCS metrics.

SemEval2020 task-9 Sentimix (Hinglish and Spanglish)

  • Dataset contains social media comments and tweets on code-mixed languages such as Hindi+English, Spanish+English.
  • Task was to classify the text into positive, negative, and netural sentiments. We achieved overall third ranking on Spanglish with F1-macro of 0.77.
  • To clean and extract features from social media data, we applied multiple preprocessing steps such as Levenshtein distance based spell correction, contraction and acronym expansion, profanity vector, User and URL replacement.
  • We used ensemble model of RNN based self-attention, CNN and LSTM based model with extensive hyper-parameter tuning.

SemEval2020 task-12 OffensEval (English, Greek, Turkish, Danish, and Arabic)

  • English task was divided in three subtasks. A) Detect offensiveness, B)Targeted or not, C)Determine the target.
  • For other languages mentioned, there was only one task- that is to detect Offensiveness in different language corpus.
  • We used different preprocessing methods for different languages from CLTK library.
  • We trained English corpus using pre-trained embeddings on Social-media corpus using Fasttext model. We trained using transformer based models on English language.
  • We trained Turkish and Danish corpus using pre-trained embeddings using stacked ensemble model.
  • We achieved F1-macro of 0.908 on English language and 0.886 on Greek language social media corpus.

Object Recognition and Classification

Worked on Object Recognition and Classification task using Deep Neural Networks. I applied Inception v3 model on Keras framework and YAD2K for drawing bounding boxes for different objects.... My work is mostly inspired by my assignment that I completed during my course on Deep Learning on Coursera. I have created my own dataset with two classes which are myself and my friend, Thamman. I trained YOLO v3 and YOLOv3_tiny architectures on my dataset to classify and draw accurate bounding boxes on our face detection. I trained the model on NVIDIA GPU for around 8 hrs and 1500 iterations. Model can Predict the class with above 90% accuracy and it is capable of drawing the bounding boxes with 70% recall, with Intersection Over union (IOU) of the bounding boxes. I plan to extend it on Image captioning so that once it is capable of predicting classes it can be fed to Recurrent Neural Networks to predict the relevant caption for the scene, which is more relevant A.I task.

Solar Energy Forecasting

The field of solar and photovoltaic (PV) forecasting is rapidly evolving. The electricity system in India faces several challenges as the energy demand is expected to grow significantly within the next decades while the domestic energy resources regarding fossil fuels are limited.... Hence it becomes vital to get more dependent upon Renewable Energy to meet the future requirements. This project report provides state of the art of this dynamic research area, focusing on solar and PV forecast of next dates with given weather data. Diverse resources are used to generate solar and PV forecasts, ranging from measured weather and PV system data to satellite and sky imagery observations of clouds which form the basis of modern weather forecasting. Electric utility companies need accurate forecasts of energy production to have the right balance of renewable and fossil fuels available. Errors in the estimates could lead to significant expenses for the utility from excess fuel consumption or emergency purchases of electricity from neighboring utilities. Power forecasts typically are derived from numerical weather prediction models, but statistical and machine learning techniques are increasingly being used in conjunction with the mathematical models to produce more accurate forecasts.

Smart Energy Metering System

This project presents the distinct way of billing and metering system of Energy meters. Electricity Supply Company can take the reading of monthly Energy consumption through their mobile phones by just sending SMS (Short Message Service) to GSM (Global System for Mobile) embedded in Energy meter. ...Features included in our proposed Smart Energy meter are-

  • Energy Department can take the reading using their mobile phone rather than sending meter reader at the end of every month.
  • They can Cut OFF the Electricity line using Relay by just sending SMS to GSM module and switch it ON again by sending an SMS.
  • A user can continuously monitor their Meter reading of particular month on LCD screen and online by logging into his account.
